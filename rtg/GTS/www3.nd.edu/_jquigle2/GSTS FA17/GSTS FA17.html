Notre Dame Graduate Student Topology Seminar

 

The goal of this seminar is to introduce participants to operads and delooping machinery. The format of the seminar was a four-part introductory minicourse followed by individual talks by participants.

Organizers: Jens Jakob Kjaer and J.D. Quigley
Seminar: Wednesday, 2:00-3:00pm, Hurley 258
Problem session: Friday, 9:30-10:30am, Hurley 258
