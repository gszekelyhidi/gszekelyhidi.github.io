Notre Dame Graduate Student Topology Seminar

 

The goal of this seminar is to introduce participants to rational homotopy theory. In the first five weeks, PJ Jedlovec will give a minicourse (notes below). The following weeks, participants will give talks and lead problem sessions on applications of rational homotopy theory to their specific area of interest.

Organizers: Jens Jakob Kjaer and J.D. Quigley
Seminar: Tuesday, 9:00-10:00am, Hayes-Healy 125
Problem session: Thursday, 4:00-5:00pm, Hayes-Healy 125
