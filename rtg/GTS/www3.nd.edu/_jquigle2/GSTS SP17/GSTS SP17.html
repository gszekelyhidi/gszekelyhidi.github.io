Notre Dame Graduate Student Topology Seminar

 

The goal of this seminar is to introduce participants to equivariant stable homotopy theory. References include May's "Alaska Notes", Schwede's equivariant notes, and Hill-Hopkins-Ravenel's papers.

Note: This webpage is still under construction, so many notes and problem links are broken
