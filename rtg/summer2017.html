Undergraduate summer workshop

July 31 - August 4, 2017

This is a week long event with a summer school component, where basic concepts in geometry and topology will be introduced, followed by a research conference aimed at more advanced undergraduates.

Part II: Advanced topics

August 2 - 4

The last three days of the workshop will consist of talks outlining more advanced topics of current research interest. In addition there will be a poster session where undergraduates can present their research. Some background in topology or geometry will be assumed from the participants.
